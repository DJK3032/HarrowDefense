Advanced Tactical Electronics

Mission

Harrow Defense builds body-worn electronics that close critical capability gaps for dismounted soldiers. We develop low-SWaP systems engineered for the tactical edge — where size, weight, power, and survivability are non-negotiable.

Our work sits at the intersection of embedded systems, mesh networking, and edge computing — purpose-built for the warfighter.
